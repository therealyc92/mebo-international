Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries

How can I purchase MEBO Cream in my country?

MEBO Cream is available through authorized distributors and hospital pharmacies in each country. Contact us indicating your location and we will connect you with the corresponding local representative.

Does MEBO Cream require a prescription?

The sales classification (prescription or over-the-counter) varies by country legislation. We recommend consulting with your local pharmacist or healthcare professional.

Do you offer training for healthcare professionals?

Yes, we have a continuing education program that includes webinars, workshops, and certified courses on MEBT technology. Visit the professionals portal for more information.

How do I report an adverse event?

Adverse events can be reported via our pharmacovigilance email: farmacovigilancia@mebo-international.com, or through the health authorities in your country.
