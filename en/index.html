Our Science

A new era in burn treatment

Moist Exposed Burn Therapy (MEBT) represents a fundamentally different paradigm: regeneration, not just scarring.

Physiological Moist Environment

Maintains an optimal environment for cell regeneration, preventing wound bed desiccation and reducing pain.

Cellular Regeneration

Stimulates keratinocyte migration and re-epithelialization, promoting the natural restoration of damaged skin.

Reduced Scarring

Physiological regeneration significantly reduces scar tissue formation compared to conventional methods.

The Process

The journey of regeneration

From the initial injury to complete recovery, MEBO accompanies every stage of the healing process.

1

Injury

After a burn, early application protects the tissue and relieves pain.

2

Application

MEBO Cream creates a moist environment that supports natural regeneration.

3

Regeneration

Keratinocytes migrate and proliferate, restoring the damaged epidermis.

4

Recovery

Regenerated skin shows less scarring and improved protective function.
